# Research Report Caveats

The underlying Research Report was not included with the Phase 1 specification,
so its complete verbatim caveat section cannot be imported yet. The following
verbatim constraints were supplied with the specification and are preserved
until the report source is available.

> CRITICAL: most metabolic + aging mechanisms are rodent-only.
> Every edge MUST declare its evidence species provenance.

> RULE: Any metabolic-disease or aging-mechanism edge defaults to its true
> species. The S6K1-KO obesity-resistance phenotype is RODENT. ITP lifespan
> extension is RODENT. PEARL healthspan signals are HUMAN but early/limited.

> RMC-6236, RMC-6291, RMC-9805 are Revolution Medicines RAS(ON) inhibitors,
> NOT mTOR bi-sterics.

> mTOR S2448 phosphorylation is attributed to S6K1 feedback (NOT AKT) and
> has no defined function.

> The canonical AKT site on TSC2 is T1462. There is NO "S1462" site.

> mTOR S2481 is an autophosphorylation site (rapamycin-insensitive),
> predominant in mTORC2 - not an external-kinase target.

The Phase 5 bi-steric viewer highlights FRB and ATP sites simultaneously as a
mechanistic composite. It does not claim that a deposited ternary bi-steric
co-complex structure exists.

The Phase 6 selectivity GNN is an architecture contract and refusal-bound
serving shell, not a validated numerical model. The committed ChEMBL snapshot
is too sparse for the registered five-target quality gates, KLIFS descriptors
are not yet ingested, the Torin2 benchmark is not measured, and the A100
no-OOM gate has not been run.

Boltz-2 output is prepared as an external handoff only. Scaffold-explorer and
resistance outputs are computational hypotheses requiring experimental
validation.

The complete source Research Report has still not been supplied. Consequently,
the project cannot claim that every report caveat has been imported verbatim.
